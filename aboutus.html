Darlyn

Who am I? Why am I doing this?

Hi, I am Darlyn Lool. I am 16 years old. I am grateful for being able to create this website. I think that homelessness is an issue that definitely needs more attention. By creating this website, we bring awareness to this issue and also give people ideas on where they can volunteer / donate and how they can help with this cause. I live in the D.C area and everywhere I go there’s so many homeless people with hunger, feeling hopeless, and a need for change. With this website we are able to make a change and help someone smile today!
