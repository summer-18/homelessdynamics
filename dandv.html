Volunteer

In Homeless Dynamics, there are varieties of options for everyone to choose from. You can volunteer to sort clothes out into sections: men, women, and children, help serve meals to the homeless, or help organize shelters so that their space can be clean. Volunteer at your nearest homeless shelter.Sign up at Homeless Dynamics and support the poor and low-income families all around the U.S to make a change today!

Donations

There are many ways in which you can support this movement like donating clothes, food, care supplies, and/or time. You can donate clothes and care supplies to local Disaster Relief Organizations, Crisis Centers, and Homeless Shelters. You can also donate products that are in boxes, cans or sealed bags, such as uncooked pasta and canned fruits and vegetables. You can donate at your local food banks and homeless shelters.
